Data Visualization Homework 2

Data

The dataset comes from Opporunity Insights which is a team at Harvard University that researches income segregation and intergenreational mobility. This specific dataset is looking at college students from the 1980, 1981 and 1982 birth cohorts and comparing their income to their parents. The original dataset has 2,202 rows which includes colleges from all over the United States, but I filtered this down to 82 rows which only include the 4-year colleges in California.

"As part of our research studies, we put out publicly available datasets that can be used by other researchers and practitioners to support their own work."

Opporunity Insights Data Policy

The different types of columns:

  1. name: Name of college.
  2. iclevel: Four-year or two-year college. Data filtered to include only 4-year colleges.
  3. state: The US state where the college is located. Data filtered to include only colleges in California.
  4. par_rank: Mean parental income rank.
  5. k_rank: Mean kid earnings rank.
  6. count: Average number of kids per cohort.
  7. type: 1 = public, 2 = private non-profit, 3 = for-profit.

Wrangling

I narrowed the data down to 4-year colleges in California. I did this by filtering the data in Tableau to only include rows were the state is CA and the iclevel is 1.

Some of the colleges in the dataset have a comma in their name, so I used tab-seperated values to format the data instead of comma-seperated values.
